# Headline Formulas & Validation Rules

> **Used by**: Copywriting Agent
> **When**: Generating and validating headlines

---

## The Headline Formula

**"The (a) most succinct statement of (b) your greatest value for (c) the greatest number of your customers."**

### The Self-Selection Principle
"Every plausible customer, upon reading the headline, should know that this site offers something of value for them."

---

## 6 Headline Patterns

### Pattern 1: Direct Value
```
{Action verb} {specific outcome} {for/with/in} {timeframe/method}
```
- "Track every hour and bill clients in seconds"
- "Design professional logos in under 5 minutes"
- "Build landing pages that convert in one afternoon"

### Pattern 2: Audience + Outcome
```
{How/The way} {target audience} {achieves desired result}
```
- "How freelancers get paid faster without chasing invoices"
- "The way smart teams manage projects without the chaos"

### Pattern 3: Problem Elimination
```
{Desired outcome}. {No/Without} {pain point}.
```
- "Beautiful websites. No coding required."
- "Accurate time tracking. Without the busywork."

### Pattern 4: Specific Claim
```
{Specific number/metric} {benefit} {proof element}
```
- "10,000+ teams ship faster with automated testing"
- "Save 5 hours every week on expense reports"

### Pattern 5: Question
```
{Question that highlights pain or desire}?
```
- "Still tracking time in spreadsheets?"
- "What if onboarding took minutes, not weeks?"

### Pattern 6: Comparison
```
{Your solution} vs {old way}: {clear winner statement}
```
- "Email that converts vs. emails that get ignored"

---

## Different Angles for Headlines

- **Address objections** directly in the headline
- **Say something your competitors can't** (unique differentiator)
- **Lead with social proof** if numbers are impressive

---

## Validation Rules - REJECT If:

### VAGUE - Could apply to any product
```
❌ "Supercharge your workflow"
❌ "Take your business to the next level"
❌ "The future of productivity"
```

### JARGONY - Buzzwords without meaning
```
❌ "AI-powered solution for modern teams"
❌ "Revolutionary platform for optimization"
❌ "Next-gen technology for digital transformation"
```

### COMPANY-FOCUSED - About you, not them
```
❌ "The world's leading project management tool"
❌ "Award-winning software since 2015"
```

### CLEVER OVER CLEAR - Sacrifices understanding
```
❌ "Time is money. We save both."
❌ "Your workflow's new best friend"
```

### FORCES USER TO WAIT
```
❌ Carousels that rotate headlines
❌ Animations that delay the message
```

## Subhead Formula

**"How you'll provide the value in the h1"**

### Approaches:
1. **Mechanism**: "With AI that learns your writing style"
2. **Social Proof**: "Join 50,000+ designers who've made the switch"
3. **Ease**: "Set up in 60 seconds. No credit card required."
4. **Outcome**: "Go from idea to published site before lunch"

---

## Headline Checklist

- [ ] States specific value (what do they GET?)
- [ ] Could NOT apply to competitors
- [ ] Target audience recognizes themselves
- [ ] Creates curiosity to read more
- [ ] Under 70 characters
- [ ] No jargon or buzzwords
- [ ] User outcome, not product features
