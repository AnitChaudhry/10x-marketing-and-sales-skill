# Welcome Email Sequence - SaaS Product

## Overview
- **Trigger:** New user signup
- **Emails:** 5
- **Duration:** 14 days
- **Goal:** Activate user, demonstrate value, convert to paid

## Flow Diagram

```
Day 0: Welcome + Quick Win
         ↓
Day 1: Origin Story + Mission
         ↓
Day 3: Best Tip (Value Bomb)
         ↓
Day 7: Case Study / Social Proof
         ↓
Day 14: Soft Pitch + Urgency
```

## Timing

| Email | Day | Subject | Purpose |
|-------|-----|---------|---------|
| 1 | 0 | Welcome + first win | Deliver value, set expectations |
| 2 | 1 | Why we built this | Build connection, share mission |
| 3 | 3 | Hidden tip most miss | Demonstrate expertise |
| 4 | 7 | How [Customer] did X | Social proof, overcome objections |
| 5 | 14 | Ready to level up? | Soft conversion pitch |

## Exit Conditions
- Purchased plan → Exit flow, enter customer onboarding
- Unsubscribed → Exit flow
- No opens after email 3 → Enter re-engagement flow
